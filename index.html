About Us

Who We Are

JD Premium Dental Clinic was founded with a powerful vision: to make premium dental care accessible, affordable, and compassionate for every resident of Najafgarh and surrounding areas.

Situated at the heart of Jatav Mohalla — conveniently located opposite the Primary Health Centre and Metro Parking Gate — our clinic has become a trusted name in dental healthcare for families across Najafgarh, New Delhi.

We understand visiting the dentist can feel intimidating. That is why every member of our team is committed to creating an environment where you feel heard, comfortable, and well cared for.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Where is JD Premium Dental Clinic located?
+
We are located at Plot No. 1467-68, Street No. 1, Jatav Mohalla, Najafgarh, New Delhi – 110043. We are situated opposite the Primary Health Centre and Metro Parking Gate, making us very easy to find by both public transport and private vehicle.
What are your clinic hours?
+
Our clinic is currently open and closes at 3:00 PM. Please call us at 087430 46085 to confirm current timings or to schedule your appointment.
How do I book an appointment?
+
You can book an appointment by calling us at 087430 46085 or by filling out the appointment form on this page. Walk-in patients are also welcome, subject to availability.
Do you treat children?
+
Yes! We provide gentle, child-friendly dental care for patients of all ages, including infants, children, and teenagers. Our pediatric dental services are designed to make kids feel comfortable and at ease.
Is root canal treatment painful?
+
With modern anaesthesia and techniques, root canal treatment is no more uncomfortable than getting a routine filling. Our dentists ensure you are completely comfortable before and throughout the entire procedure.
How often should I visit the dentist?
+
We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for a routine check-up and professional cleaning, even if you have no dental problems. Early detection prevents costly and complex treatments later.
Do you offer teeth whitening?
+
Yes, we offer professional in-clinic teeth whitening treatments that are safe, effective, and deliver noticeably brighter results compared to any over-the-counter products.
What should I do in a dental emergency?
+
If you are experiencing severe tooth pain, a broken tooth, or any other dental emergency, please call us immediately at 087430 46085. We will advise you and arrange to see you as soon as possible.
